# Pages

## Core routes
- `/` home and topical authority hub
- `/apps/` app hubs for iOS, macOS, Android
- `/devices/` device hubs and model pages
- `/comparisons/` programmatic comparisons
- `/guides/` setup and how-to guides
- `/troubleshooting/` issue-specific fixes
- `/blog/` long-form editorial updates

## Programmatic pages
- App and device pages are generated from `src/data/` datasets.
- Markdown collections generate guides, troubleshooting, and blog posts.
