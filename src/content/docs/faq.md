## Do you sell IPTV subscriptions?
No. We do not sell subscriptions or host streams.

## Why is my EPG missing?
Start with the EPG troubleshooting guide and confirm your time zone and EPG URL.

## Which iOS app should I use?
We recommend Jamrun IPTV for Apple devices.

## How often do app pages update?
App metadata is refreshed on a biweekly schedule, with editorial updates as needed.
