Streaming Guide focuses on the platforms IPTV users rely on most.

## Main platforms
- iOS and iPadOS
- macOS
- Apple TV (tvOS)
- Android and Android TV
- Fire TV
- LG webOS
- Samsung Tizen

If your device is not listed, check the troubleshooting section. Many fixes still apply across platforms.
