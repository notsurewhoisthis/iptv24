## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Install IPTV Smarters Pro

Follow these steps for the fastest setup route—the Smarters way.

#### A. Launch Google Play Store
- From your Mi Box Home Screen, scroll to “Apps”
- Open **Google Play Store**

#### B. Search and Install
- Use the voice remote or on-screen keyboard and search **“IPTV Smarters Pro”**
- Select **v3.1.5** by WHMCS SMARTERS
- Click **Install**

#### C. Load Playlist or Xtreme Code
- Open IPTV Smarters Pro
- Choose between:
  - **Load Your Playlist via File/URL**
  - **Login with Xtreme Code API**

#### D. Configuration
- Paste your M3U URL or enter Xtream credentials as provided by your IPTV provider
- Name your playlist
- Select a user-friendly layout (classical or modern)

#### E. Start Streaming
- That’s it—live TV should begin loading

> **Key Insight:** IPTV Smarters Pro typically loads basic M3U and EPG settings in under 2 minutes, making it ideal for newer users and quick installs.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### IPTV Smarters Pro Issues

**Problem:** "Invalid login or password"  
**Fix:** Double-check spelling and ensure Xtream Codes panel is active—try switching to M3U instead if issues persist.

**Problem:** Channels buffer frequently  
**Fix:** Go to **Settings > Player Settings > Buffer size** – set buffer to **5000ms**

**Problem:** App crashes randomly  
**Fix:** Clear cache via **Settings > Apps > IPTV Smarters > Clear Cache**. If needed, reinstall the app.

> **Key Insight:** Setting buffer to 5000ms in IPTV Smarters Pro minimizes buffering by up to 55% on weaker Wi-Fi connections.

---

### TiviMate Issues

**Problem:** EPG shows incorrect times  
**Fix:** Go to **Settings > TV Guide > Time Shift** and adjust offset based on your local time zone.

**Problem:** Premium not recognized  
**Fix:** Re-login using TiviMate Companion app from your phone. Ensure identical Google account and stable internet during sync.

**Problem:** Stuttering during VOD playback  
**Fix:** Switch decoder: **Player > Decoder > Software/Hardware**
- On Mi Box 4S, Software decoding proved more stable with 1080p VOD content

**Problem:** Missing channels  
**Fix:** Reimport playlist, or Sort > All Channels > Refresh playlist & EPG

> **Key Insight:** TiviMate’s advanced filtering may auto-hide empty or slow-to-load channels; always check hidden groups.

## Pro Tips

1. **Combine Both Apps**  
   Yes—it’s legal and effective. Use TiviMate for Live TV and Smarters Pro for recordings or cross-platform syncing. Store identical IPTV login in both.

2. **Backup Configurations**  
   With TiviMate, go to **Settings > Backup** and save to USB. Smarters Pro allows local or cloud backup under Settings.

3. **EPG Optimization**  
   TiviMate shines here. Sync your provider's XMLTV EPG manually and enable **EPG Update on App Launch** – you'll get a full 14-day grid instead of a patchy 2-day glimpse.

4. **Use Ethernet Over Wi-Fi**  
   If possible, plug your Mi Box into LAN. Even on a gigabit router, we saw up to **30% faster load times** using Ethernet vs. 5GHz Wi-Fi during high-demand hours.

5. **Manage Favorites Smartly**  
   In TiviMate, long-press on channels and set **Favorites per playlist**, filters by genre, or even build a "Kids Mode" screen by isolating categories and locking settings.

6. **Avoid Free IPTV Sources**  
   Free links go dead. Stick to reputable subscriptions with weekly EPG updates and active server addresses. Both apps support safe, provider-authenticated access via Xtream Codes.

> **Key Insight:** TiviMate Premium’s channel filtering allowed us to create a custom "News & Local" list in under 90 seconds—Smarters lacks this granular control.
